How MoveIt protects your data and respects your rights.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At MoveIt, your trust is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data while ensuring transparency and safety in how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect details such as your name, contact information, address, and payment details when you use our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us deliver moving services, process payments, improve our logistics, and provide timely updates.

3. Data Security

We follow strict security practices to safeguard your data. While no system is 100% secure, we constantly improve our safeguards to protect you.

4. Sharing of Information

We never sell your data. We only share it with trusted partners such as payment processors and logistics teams when necessary.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us for any privacy-related concerns.

6. Policy Updates

Our Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted here with the effective date.
