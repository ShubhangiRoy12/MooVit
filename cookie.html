Understanding how MoveIt uses cookies.

Our Use of Cookies

MoveIt uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance website performance and improve user experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember preferences and improve functionality.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to analyze website traffic, improve services, remember user preferences, and enhance overall performance.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

- Essential Cookies (required for site functionality)
- Performance Cookies (analytics & traffic monitoring)
- Functional Cookies (user preferences storage)

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some trusted third-party services (such as analytics providers) may place cookies to collect anonymous usage statistics.
